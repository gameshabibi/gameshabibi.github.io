Refunds and Cancellation

What to expect if a Games Habibi order needs review or reversal.

This page explains the circumstances under which refunds may be reviewed and when cancellations are no longer possible for digital items.

Refunds require valid proof Requests should be timely Digital orders may be irreversible after delivery

Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed in accordance with our refund policy. To initiate a refund, you must contact us within 7 days of the transaction and provide valid proof.

Refunds may be considered if

  • The product or service was defective
  • The order was not delivered
  • The delivered product was materially different from what was described
  • The request is legitimate and made within the stated time

Cancellation Policy

Since our products are digital and usually delivered quickly, cancellations are not possible after payment once delivery has begun. However, if you contact us immediately after purchase and before delivery, cancellation may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

How to Request Help

Email mr.bgmi9098@gmail.com with your order details, issue summary, and payment proof. Including your order ID will help speed up the review.

Before You Submit a Request

Make sure you have the details needed for support to verify your case.

Recommended details

  • Your order ID
  • Payment screenshot
  • What went wrong and when it happened
